Two Singles Coming Soon

March 25, 2026

Music is my way of coping with whatever life throws at me. This week I’m dropping two singles under my own name, Kevin Raaijmakers, and they couldn’t be more different from each other. I don’t play any instruments or sing. But I am still able to tell my story using ‘Music Generation’ and predefining the lyrics once I feel comfortable.

Running in Circles

The first one is a trap rap track. It’s the one that’s closest to dropping, so keep an eye out, it should be out any day now. The title says it all, really.

Medicated or Be Hated

The second track leans more into rock. It’s called Medicated or Be Hated and will follow shortly after. A bit of a heavier vibe compared to the first one.
